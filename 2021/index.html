ShowOHI/O

Ohio State's annual tech innovation and entrepreneurship showcase.
Friday, April 9, 2021 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM

This black tie event highlights outstanding and innovative technologies developed by Ohio State faculty, students, and alumni. ShowOHI/O brings together invested parties from across Buckeye Nation for an evening that celebrates and supports tech entrepreneurs making an impact.
